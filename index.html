Guilherme Maeda
Researcher in Robotics at Preferred Networks Inc.

Eventually, our society will depend on robots as general-purpose helpers that will accompany us throughout our entire lives. This vision requires robots capable of learning skills by themselves to manipulate and interact with the world in a way that is useful for us. My work involves the investigation and design of learning control methods, and their implementation and validation using real robots.
